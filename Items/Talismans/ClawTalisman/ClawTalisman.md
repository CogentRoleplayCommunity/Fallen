![Copyrighted Image](ClawTalisman.png)

## Desc

A talisman depicting a claw and an assassin.

## Info

Item Type: Talisman

Tier: Flawless (+6)

## Effects

### Ariel Assassin

While performing an aerial attack, one of the dice from the "High Ground" modifier becomes an automatic win.
