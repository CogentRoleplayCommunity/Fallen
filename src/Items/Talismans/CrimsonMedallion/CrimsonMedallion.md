# Crimson Medallion

![Copyrighted Image](CrimsonMedallion.png)

## Desc

A medallion with Crimson amber inlaid. A precious talisman for protecting one's life.

Boosts maximum vitality.

## Info

Item Type: Talisman

Tier: Flawless (+6)

## Effects

### Enhanced Vitality

Reduce up to 1d6 of total injury penalty dice.
