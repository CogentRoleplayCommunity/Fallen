# Blade Barrier

You touch a creature, until it's next turn it has minor resistance to bludgeon, pierce, slash, hew, and shockwave damage.

## Info

Range: Melee

Engaeable Opponents: Focused

Magic Resource Required: 0

INT Requirement: 0

Runes Required:

- Gravity Rune (lvl -1)
- Body Rune (lvl -1)
